## Usage Guidelines

### When Planning Content:
1. Check both competitor categories' approaches to the topic
2. Identify how [Company Name] provides unique value
3. Position between the two extremes when applicable
4. Emphasize your unique differentiators
5. Focus on your target audience's specific use cases

### When Writing Content:
1. Acknowledge alternative approaches when relevant
2. Position [Company Name] based on genuine differentiation
3. Use concrete examples showing limitations of each approach
4. Demonstrate [Company Name]'s unique value
5. Always be fair and factual about competitors

### When Analyzing Performance:
1. Track rankings against both competitor types
2. Monitor adoption trends in your market
3. Identify shifting sentiment and buyer priorities
4. Assess where [Company Name]'s differentiation resonates most
5. Double down on unique positioning
