### Notes on manual-fill slides
These slides have too many repeated cells (rows in tables, logo slots, team members) for practical token replacement. They get a LAYOUT name so they can be included/excluded from a deck, but their content is filled directly in Google Slides:
- `COMPARE_2COL` (slide 34): 12 "Row item" cells
- `TEAM` (slide 35): 8 name + 8 role boxes
- `COMMERCIALS` (slide 37): 18 "Row item" cells
- `APPENDIX_TABLE` (slide 39): 21 "Row item" cells
- `CUSTOMERS_A/B` (slides 41-42): 12 "Logo" boxes each

### BFSI deck building guidance
Typical sales deck flow:
1. `BFSI_TITLE_A` — Customer name + date
2. `AGENDA` — 4-5 topics
3. `STATEMENT` — Bold opening claim
4. `PROBLEM` — Their pain point
5. `SOLUTION` — Your answer
6. `OUTCOMES_METRICS` — 3 headline numbers
7. `DIFFERENTIATORS` — Why [Company Name] specifically
8. `CASE_STUDY_3COL` or `CUSTOMER_QUOTE` — Proof
9. `PILOT` — How to start
10. `THANK_YOU_A` — Close with contact info

Adjust based on audience: add `WHY_NOW`/`STAKES` for executive audiences, `INTEGRATION`/`CONTROLS` for technical audiences, `PILLAR_*` deep-dives when you have more time.
